    Create `app/assets/scss/abstracts/_animations.scss` with:
    1. Custom cubic-bezier easing curves:

       - `$ease-out-expo: cubic-bezier(0.19, 1, 0.22, 1)` - dramatic ease-out for reveals

       - `@keyframes scale-in` - scale(0.95) → scale(1) with fade
    3. Animation mixins:
       - `@mixin animate-fade($duration: 300ms, $ease: $ease-smooth)`
